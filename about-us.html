About R.C. Ramos
Construction Corporation

Established in 1995, R.C. Ramos Construction Corporation (RCRCC) is a highly regarded Triple "A" Construction Firm, specializing
in general construction and design-build services. Over the years, we have built a reputation as a responsive, cost-effective, and
innovative leader in the Building and Construction Industry.

Our success is driven by a team of skilled architects, engineers, and planners, making RCRCC a sought-after firm in the Design and
Construction sector. We are committed to excellence, efficiency, and timely project delivery, ensuring that every construction
endeavor meets the highest standards.

With the combined expertise of our design engineers, architects, and field engineers, RCRCC has consistently delivered high-
quality projects across hospitals, industrial facilities, malls, schools, residential developments, and commercial establishments.
Our dedication to nation-building and client satisfaction remains at the core of everything we do.

Today, RCRCC continues to expand its legacy, partnering with property developers, businesses, and fellow constructors to shape
the future of Philippine infrastructure. We are your trusted partner in building and developing your vision into reality.

R.C. Ramos Construction Corporation: A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995, R.C. Ramos Construction Corporation (RCRCC) has built a strong foundation of trust, innovation, and quality craftsmanship in the Philippine construction industry. Guided by the leadership of Rodrigo C. Ramos and Engr. Damian R. Ramos, the company grew into a Triple 'A' accredited construction firm, delivering high-profile projects across residential, commercial, industrial, healthcare, and government sectors. Today, RCRCC continues its legacy of excellence, combining decades of expertise with modern construction technologies and innovative methodologies. With a commitment to integrity, client satisfaction, and nation-building, we remain a trusted partner in shaping the future of Philippine infrastructure.

FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our services and how we engage with clients.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including general construction, design, and engineering. Our team is dedicated to delivering high-quality projects tailored to meet your specific needs. Whether it's residential, commercial, or industrial construction, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

How does the process work?

Our process begins with a consultation to understand your project requirements. We then provide a detailed proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and budget. Once approved, our team will manage the project from start to finish, ensuring quality and efficiency.

What is your timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. During the consultation, we will provide an estimated timeline tailored to your project. Our team is committed to adhering to deadlines while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, we offer warranties on our workmanship and materials used in your project. This ensures your investment is protected and gives you peace of mind. Details of the warranty will be provided in your project contract.

How can I contact you?

ou can reach us through our contact form on the website, or by calling our office directly. Our team is ready to assist you with any inquiries you may have. We look forward to hearing from you!
